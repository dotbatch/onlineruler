Online Ruler — Actual Size in cm, mm & Inches

A free virtual ruler that measures objects on your screen at real size. The scale runs along the top and left edges at once, so you can read width and height together — just drag the orange block to wrap your object. Works on any phone, tablet or desktop.

How to calibrate the ruler

Screens differ in pixel density, so calibrate once for true actual-size accuracy. Tap Calibrate, lay a bank or credit card on the screen and match the orange bar to it. The setting is saved on this device. Tap the lock 🔒 afterwards so you don't bump Calibrate by accident.

Use your phone as a ruler

Once calibrated, place your object in the top-left corner and drag the handles until the block covers it. The live width (horizontal) and height (vertical) update instantly in cm or inches.
